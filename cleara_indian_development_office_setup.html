Risk Management & Mitigation

High Priority Risks

Talent Retention

Risk of losing key contract developers to competitors

Mitigation: Competitive compensation, clear career path, performance bonuses

Project Delays

Potential delays due to learning curve and integration

Mitigation: Comprehensive onboarding, mentorship program, buffer time in planning

Medium Priority Risks

Cost Overruns

Potential budget exceeding due to unforeseen expenses

Mitigation: 10% contingency budget, monthly cost reviews, strict approval process

Quality Concerns

Maintaining quality with mixed team experience levels

Mitigation: Strict code review process, automated testing, regular quality audits
