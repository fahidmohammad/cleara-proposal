Executive Summary

Product Vision

ClearaProvider is a comprehensive service provider platform designed specifically for the GCC market, enabling professional service teams to efficiently manage job assignments, coordinate with property owners, execute high-quality services, and build sustainable business relationships through a technology-driven ecosystem.

Target Market

  • Primary: Professional cleaning and maintenance service providers in GCC countries
  • Secondary: Individual contractors and specialized service professionals
  • Tertiary: Facility management companies and corporate service teams

Key Value Propositions

Mobile-First Workflow: Optimized for field service management and real-time coordination
Quality Assurance: Built-in documentation and verification systems
GCC Market Optimization: Cultural, regulatory, and business practice alignment
Business Intelligence: Performance analytics and growth insights
