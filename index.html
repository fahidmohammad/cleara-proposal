Executive Summary

This proposal presents Cleara, a comprehensive property services platform designed specifically for the GCC market. Cleara addresses the fundamental challenges in property management by connecting property owners with verified service providers through a subscription-based model, ensuring predictable costs, quality assurance, and cultural compliance.
