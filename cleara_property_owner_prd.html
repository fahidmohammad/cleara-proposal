Executive Summary

Product Vision

Cleara Property Owner App is a comprehensive property management platform designed specifically for the GCC market, enabling property owners to seamlessly manage their real estate portfolios, coordinate professional services, and optimize property operations through subscription-based service packages.

Target Market

  • Primary: Individual property owners (1-10 properties) in GCC countries
  • Secondary: Small property management companies (10-50 properties)
  • Tertiary: Real estate investors and property developers

Key Value Propositions

Subscription-Based Service Access: Predictable costs with quality-assured service delivery
Real-Time Service Coordination: Live tracking and communication with verified service providers
GCC Market Optimization: Cultural, religious, and regulatory compliance
Portfolio Intelligence: Data-driven insights for property optimization
